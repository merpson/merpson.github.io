History and Character Background

Funky Kong is a character in the Mario Kart series, first appearing as a playable character in Mario Kart Wii. He is a laid-back, surfer-dude gorilla who runs the Funky Stadium course in the game.

Stats and Abilities

Funky Kong is a heavyweight character in Mario Kart Wii with high speed and acceleration, but low handling and off-road abilities. His special item is the Flame Runner, a high-speed motorcycle that is one of the best vehicles in the game.

Media

Fun Facts

  • Funky Kong is voiced by Damon D'Oliveira in Mario Kart Wii.
  • He is also a playable character in Donkey Kong Country: Tropical Freeze.
  • Funky Kong's appearance in Mario Kart Wii was a surprise to many fans, as he had not appeared in a Mario game before.
